Adishesha Sai Nandkoomar

Game Developer specialising in programming, 3D modelling, and interactive storytelling.

Background

Born on February 9th, 2007, I discovered my passion for game development at the age of four when introduced to the Professor Layton series, sparking both creativity and curiosity.

Inspirations

I draw inspiration from beloved titles that shaped my creative outlook:

  • Pokémon franchise (especially HeartGold and SoulSilver)
  • Inazuma Eleven series (particularly Inazuma Eleven 3: Bomb Blast)
  • Ace Attorney games (with Trials and Tribulations as my all-time favourite)

In game development, I deeply admire Satoru Iwata and Akihiro Hino, embodying the creativity and passion I aspire to carry forward.

Education & Leadership

In 2024, I matriculated as Deputy Head Boy of St. Dominics Newcastle, earning distinctions in Information Technology, Life Orientation, and Computer Applications Technology. I'm now completing my first year in Game Design and Development, refining my skills in interactive storytelling at Vega School.

Mission

I believe in using games as a means to connect, inspire, and bring joy to others. My dream is to create experiences that leave smiles on faces across the globe, just as the games I grew up with once did for me.
