About Me

The creative mind behind Spectra Studios

Adishesha Nandkoomar

Game Developer

"My mission is to create games that resonate with players on an emotional level, blending technical excellence with artistic vision."

My Journey

I'm a passionate game developer with a background in both programming and design. My journey began at age 4 with the Professor Layton series, which introduced me to gaming and left a lasting impact on my appreciation for puzzles and storytelling.

With expertise in Unity Engine and programming languages like C# and Java, I specialise in bringing creative concepts to life through creative code and engaging gameplay mechanics.

My Inspirations

I'm deeply passionate about narrative design, drawing inspiration from my favourite films like Toy Story 2 and Lilo & Stitch, as well as the entire James Bond series (having watched all 25 films). These stories have shaped my understanding of character development, emotional resonance, and the power of a compelling narrative.

My Approach

I believe that great games are built at the intersection of technical excellence and creative vision. My development process emphasises the process of player-centric design, thoughtful implementation of game mechanics, alongside the integration of compelling narratives.

Whether working independently or leading a team, I prioritise clear communication, collaborative problem-solving, and empathy for both the player experience and the development process.
