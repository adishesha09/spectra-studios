My Background

I am a first-year Game Design and Development student at Vega School, driven by a lifelong passion for interactive storytelling and immersive experiences. Introduced to gaming at age four through the Professor Layton series, I discovered my calling early: to craft worlds that inspire the same joy I felt holding a Nintendo DS as a child.

With five years of self-taught development experience - beginning at age 13 with small-scale projects, I combine technical skills in coding and design with a deep understanding of player engagement. While game development remains my core focus, I actively explore adjacent fields like web development to broaden my expertise in technology.

Outside of game development, I am an avid film fanatic with a deep appreciation for the magic of cinema, particularly films produced by The Walt Disney Company, the Marvel Cinematic Universe, and the Star Wars franchise. My favourite Disney titles include Toy Story, the original Lilo & Stitch (2002), The Lion King, and Peter Pan. I'm also a passionate fan of the Iron Man trilogy, with Iron Man 2 standing out as my personal favourite.

My mission is to create games that spark wonder and connect players across the globe through memorable interactive experiences.
